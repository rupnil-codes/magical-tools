# magical-tools

### 1. Gravity Apple
Custom recipie
- Eating time: 0.5s
- Gives slow falling 2 for 10sec

### 2. Teleporter (Item)
Custom recipe
- Teleports to 16 block in the direction you are looking
- Cooldown 6 sec
- 10 uses allowed

### 3. Rabbit Shoes (Armor):
Custom recipe
- Increases jump height
- Gives speed buff
- Negates all fall damage

### 4. Vacuum Pouch
Custom recipe
- Attracts all nearby items towards the player (within 24 block radius)
- Attraction speed is 0.3f
- Works passively in off or main hand
- Particle Effects
